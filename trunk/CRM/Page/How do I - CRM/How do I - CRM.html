1. How do I create a Customer?

a. To make a Customer, click on the Customer Link on the Setup Page or click here

2. What is a “Customer Group” and how do I create it?

a. Customer Group helps to differentiate your customers under different categories as you want.

b. To make a Customer Group, click on the Customer Group Link on the Setup Page or click here

3. What is a “Territory” and how do I create it?

a. Territory helps you analyze your sales region wise.

b. To make a Territory, click on the Territory Link on the Setup Page or click here

4. How do I set targets?

a. Targets can be set for a territory, sales person or a sales partner.

b. Open territory, sales person or a sales partner, specify the targets you want to set in the target details table and save.

5. What is a Price List?

a. You can specify standard rates for each item against a price list.

b. For example, there are four price lists - North, South, East and West. In an item master , under the tab Sales Details, you can select the relevant price lists and mention the reference rates against them.
