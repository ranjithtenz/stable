1. How do I make a Sales Invoice?

a. To make a Sales Invoice, click on the Sales Invoice Link on the Accounts Page or click here

b. Select your Customer and optionally add Items that you sell.

2. How do I make accept a Payment?

a. To accept a payment against a Sales Invoice, find the invoice and click on "Make Bank Voucher" button in the "Next Steps" Tab

b. To accept a payment as advance, create a new Journal Voucher, select type as "Bank Voucher", In your "debit" row, remember to set "Is Advance" column as "Yes"

3. How do I book an Expense (or a Supplier Invoice)?

a. Make a new Purchase Invoice

4. How do I enter Opening Entries?

a. See the detailed help page for Making Opening Entries

5. Where do I see my Profit & Loss Statement?

a. Open Financial Statements from Accounts module, select Statement --> Profit and Loss.

6. How do I setup my Chart of Accounts?

a. To setup your chart of accounts, click on "Chart of Accounts" from the Setup page or click here.

b. Explore the pre-created Chart of Accounts for your convenience.

c. To add a Child Account or edit details, click on the Account on the tree.

7. How do I create Tax Heads?

a. To create Tax heads, open your Chart of Accounts

b. Explore to the group in Liablities (Sources of Funds) where you want to add your tax heads. Your accountant can help you where to put this.

c. Select your group and create a new Account Head, select "Account Type" as "Tax" and optionally set the tax rate

8. Where do i create my credit and debit notes?

a. A journal voucher can be made for this purpose by selecting the voucher type as - credit note or debit note as per requirement.
